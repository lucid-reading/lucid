How to use Lucid
Reading modes
WordOne word at a time with ORP focus alignment. Best for maximum speed.
SentenceFull sentence displayed, word highlighted as it plays. Good for comprehension.
ParaFull paragraph visible with word tracking. Great for context-heavy material.
Page~220 words visible at once, current word highlighted. Natural reading feel.
Flagging & export
◆ FlagTap the Flag button (or press F) to mark the current passage. A counter badge appears on the export button in the top bar.
↓ ExportTap the export button (top-right, shown when you have flags) to download all your flagged passages as a .txt file — perfect for notes, highlights, and review.
TipYou can also export your flags from the Finished screen at the end of a session.
Top bar buttons
☰Chapter navigator — jump between chapters in ePub files.
◷Reading intelligence — session stats, WPM history, and insights.
◑Theme toggle — cycles between dark, sepia, and light.
⚙Environment panel — binaural beats, ambient light, breathe, noise masking.
Look up any word
Double tapDouble-tap (or double-click) any word in any mode to look up its definition instantly.
Keyboard shortcuts

Tips
Size ±Adjusts font size across all reading modes simultaneously.
AmbientEdge glow shifts warm (simple text) or cool (dense/complex text) automatically.
URLPaste any web URL or Wikipedia link to read the article directly.
